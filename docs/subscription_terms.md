# TempCam Subscription Terms

Last updated: March 27, 2026

## 1. Subscription Plan

TempCam offers one subscription plan:

- `TempCam Yearly Access`
- billed as an auto-renewing yearly subscription

## 2. Price

The intended default price for the first release is:

- `$3.00 / year`

Actual localized pricing may vary by country or region as configured in Google Play or the App Store.

## 3. Free Trial

- TempCam may offer a store-managed 15-day free trial for eligible users.
- Trial eligibility is determined by Google Play or the App Store, not by TempCam alone.
- If a trial is started and not canceled before the end of the trial period, the subscription renews into the paid yearly plan according to the platform store rules.

## 4. Billing

- Payment is charged by the platform store at confirmation of purchase or after any applicable free trial ends.
- The subscription renews automatically unless canceled before the renewal date.
- The platform store manages billing, renewals, refunds, and cancellation policies.

## 5. Access

- An active subscription or eligible active store-managed free trial is required to use TempCam.
- If the subscription expires or is not renewed, access to the app can be restricted until the subscription is restored or purchased again.

## 6. Restoration

Users can restore eligible purchases through the app's restore flow when reinstalling the app or changing devices, subject to the rules of the platform store.

## 7. Cancellation

Users can manage or cancel the subscription through:

- Google Play subscriptions
- Apple App Store subscriptions

Canceling a subscription stops future renewals but generally does not revoke access before the current billing period ends.

## 8. Changes

Subscription pricing, benefits, or availability may change in future versions, subject to platform store rules and applicable law.
