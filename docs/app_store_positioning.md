# TempCam Store Positioning

Last updated: April 5, 2026

This is the recommended App Store and Play Store positioning for TempCam based on the current feature set.

## Positioning Statement

TempCam is a temporary camera for sensitive photos, videos, and documents.
It helps users capture something private, act on it quickly, and let it disappear instead of leaving it in the main gallery.

## Primary Audience

- people capturing receipts, labels, notes, business cards, and travel details
- users who need a private camera space that does not clutter the main gallery
- users who want short-lived storage for sensitive photos or videos

## Tagline

`Capture. Use. Let It Disappear.`

## Subtitle

`Temporary camera for sensitive capture`

## App Store Short Description

`Capture private photos, videos, and documents outside your main gallery, then let them expire on your schedule.`

## Full Description

TempCam is built for sensitive capture.

Use it when you need to save a receipt, address, phone number, label, travel detail, private photo, or short video without leaving it in your normal gallery forever.

TempCam keeps capture local first:

- take private photos and videos outside the main gallery
- import existing media into TempCam when you need temporary storage
- detect phone numbers and addresses from documents
- call, add contacts, or open maps before saving
- choose a self-destruct timer with `Temp Save`
- protect the app with biometric lock, session privacy mode, and protected recents preview

Why TempCam is different:

- it is designed for temporary sensitive capture, not long-term gallery storage
- it helps you use information quickly, then keep it only as long as needed
- it keeps clutter and risk out of your main photo library

Good use cases:

- receipts and handwritten notes
- delivery labels and package details
- addresses and phone numbers
- business cards and travel details
- private photos or short videos you do not want sitting in the gallery
