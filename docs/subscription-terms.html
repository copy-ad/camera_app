TempCam Subscription Terms

Last updated: April 5, 2026

1. Subscription Plan

  • TempCam Yearly Access
  • Auto-renewing yearly subscription

2. Price

The intended default price for the current release is $3.00 per year. Localized pricing may vary by country or region.

3. What Access Includes

  • Temporary photo and video saving
  • Self-destruct timers
  • Import into TempCam
  • Private vault storage inside the app
  • Document action flow for detected phone numbers and addresses
  • Privacy tools such as biometric protection and related access controls, where supported

4. Free Trial

  • TempCam may offer a store-managed 15-day free trial for eligible users.
  • Trial eligibility is determined by Google Play or the App Store.
  • If the trial is not canceled before the end of the trial period, the subscription renews into the paid yearly plan according to store rules.

5. Billing

  • Payment is charged by the platform store at confirmation of purchase or after any applicable free trial ends.
  • The subscription renews automatically unless canceled before the renewal date.
  • The platform store manages billing, renewals, refunds, and cancellation policies.

6. Access and Expiration

  • An active subscription or eligible active store-managed free trial may be required to use paid TempCam features.
  • If the subscription expires or is not renewed, access to paid features can be restricted until the subscription is restored or purchased again.

7. Restoration

Users can restore eligible purchases through the app's restore flow when reinstalling the app or changing devices, subject to platform store rules.

8. Cancellation

  • Google Play subscriptions
  • Apple App Store subscriptions

Canceling stops future renewals but generally does not revoke access before the current billing period ends.

9. Changes

Subscription pricing, included benefits, or availability may change in future versions, subject to platform store rules and applicable law.
